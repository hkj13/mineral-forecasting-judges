## Strategic Overview

India faces a critical challenge in securing supply chains for essential minerals that power the nation's transition to clean energy, advanced manufacturing, and defense capabilities. This platform provides data-driven intelligence to support strategic decision-making.

### Key Findings

| Mineral | Import Dependency | YoY Growth | Strategic Risk | Primary Concern |
|---------|-------------------|------------|----------------|-----------------|

| **Lithium** | 99% | +35% | Critical | EV supply chain vulnerability |

## Trade Balance Analysis (FY2020-24)

### Cumulative Trade Deficit: $18.5 Billion

- **Copper**: $17.5B deficit (largest by volume)
- **Lithium**: $3.2B deficit (fastest growing)
- **Graphite**: $1.6B deficit (highest concentration risk)

### Concerning Trends

1. Critical mineral imports **doubled** from $3.03B (2020-21) to $8.01B (2023-24)
2. Exports **declined** from $5.00B to $3.99B in the same period
3. Lithium dependency remains at **100%** despite domestic discoveries

## Policy Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (0-12 months)

1. **Diversify Lithium Sources**: Reduce China dependency from 42% to <30%
2. **Strategic Stockpiling**: Build 6-month buffer for critical minerals
3. **Accelerate J&K Lithium Exploration**: Fast-track 5.9M MT reserve development

### Medium-Term Strategy (1-3 years)

1. **Recycling Infrastructure**: Establish urban mining capabilities for battery materials
2. **Trade Agreements**: Secure bilateral agreements with Chile, Australia, Argentina
3. **Processing Capacity**: Develop domestic refining for copper and graphite

### Long-Term Vision (3-7 years)

1. **NCMM Implementation**: Fully execute ₹34,300 crore mission objectives
2. **Technology Partnerships**: Joint ventures for deep-sea mining and REE processing
3. **Circular Economy**: Achieve 30% supply from recycled materials by 2030

## Risk Mitigation Scenarios

Our scenario simulator projects the following outcomes:

### Scenario: China Import Ban (Lithium)
- **Impact**: 42% supply reduction
- **Trade Value Change**: -$340M annually
- **Recovery Time**: 18-24 months with alternative sourcing

### Scenario: 20% Global Price Spike
- **Impact**: $1.6B additional import cost
- **Downstream Effect**: 8-12% cost increase for EV batteries
- **Mitigation**: Strategic reserves activation

### Scenario: Domestic Production Ramp (+30% annually)
- **Timeline to 50% Dependency**: 8 years for Graphite, 12+ years for Lithium
- **Investment Required**: ₹25,000 crore over 5 years
- **Employment Impact**: 50,000+ direct jobs
